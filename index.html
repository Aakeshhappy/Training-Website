Easy Abs Exercises

Crunches

Crunches are a classic exercise to target the abdominal muscles. Lie flat on your back, bend your knees, and bring your upper body towards your knees.

Leg Raises

Leg raises help in building lower abs. Lie flat and slowly raise your legs straight up, keeping your arms by your side for support.

Planks

Planks engage your entire core. Hold a push-up position, keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels, for a set time.

Easy Legs Exercises

Squats

Squats work the thighs, glutes, and calves. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, then lower your body as if sitting in a chair and return to standing.

Lunges

Lunges target the glutes and quads. Take a step forward and lower your body into a lunge, then push back to the starting position.

Calf Raises

Calf raises strengthen the calves. Stand tall and push through your toes to raise your heels off the ground, then lower back down.
